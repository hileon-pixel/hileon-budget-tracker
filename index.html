Features

Everything to manage your money

From daily expense tracking to professional invoicing and project budgeting — Hileon handles it all in one app, completely offline.

Spending Charts

Beautiful area charts and donut breakdowns showing exactly where your money goes — by day, month, quarter or year.

Budget Tracking

Set monthly budgets per category. Ring indicators and comparison badges show exactly how you're tracking.

Professional Invoicing

Create, send and track invoices with auto-generated numbers, line items, tax calculations and PDF export.

Works 100% Offline

All data stored on your device. No Wi-Fi, no mobile data, no problem. Your finances work when internet doesn't.

150+ Currencies

Full support for ZWL, ZAR, KES, MZN, ETB and 145+ more. Search and switch currencies instantly in settings.

P&L Tracker

Monitor gross profit, net profit, VAT reserve and corporate tax — everything a small business needs.

Three Modes

Personal, Business & Project

Switch between three powerful modes any time. Your data stays separate, organised and beautiful.

Personal

Track daily life spending with clarity. Know exactly where every dollar, rand or shilling goes.

  • 8 expense categories
  • Monthly budget rings & charts
  • 12-month budget editor
  • Savings summary by period
  • Full transaction history

Business

Full business suite — invoicing, inventory, P&L and multi-profile support.

  • Professional invoice system
  • Inventory management
  • P&L tracker with tax reserve
  • Multiple business profiles
  • CSV export & import

Project

Track budgets, phases and funding for any project — construction, renovation, events and more.

  • Project budget tracking
  • Phase management
  • Contribution & funding sources
  • Savings goals
  • Business cost linking
