Features

Everything you need to manage your money

From daily expense tracking to professional invoicing — Hileon handles it all in one app.

📊

Spending charts

Beautiful area charts and donut breakdowns showing exactly where your money goes by day, month, quarter or year.

🎯

Budget tracking

Set monthly budgets per category. Get comparison badges showing how you're doing vs the previous period.

🧾

Professional invoicing

Create, send and track invoices with auto-generated numbers, line items, tax calculations and PDF export.

📶

Works offline

All data stored on your device. No Wi-Fi, no mobile data, no problem. Your finances work when internet doesn't.

💱

150+ currencies

Full support for ZWL, ZAR, KES, MZN, ETB and 145+ more. Search and switch currencies instantly.

📈

P&L tracker

Monitor gross profit, net profit, VAT reserve and corporate tax — everything a small business needs.

Two modes

Personal finance & Business — in one app

Switch between Personal and Business mode any time. Your data stays separate and organised.

Personal mode

Track your daily life spending with clarity. Know exactly where every dollar, rand or shilling goes.

  • 8 expense categories — Food, Transport, Housing, Health, Shopping, Airtime, Education, Other
  • Daily, monthly, quarterly and annual period views
  • 12-month budget accordion editor
  • Savings summary — this month, quarter and year
  • Full transaction history with period and category filters
  • Edit and delete any transaction

Business mode

  • Revenue and expense tabs with period filters
  • Professional invoice system with PDF export
  • Auto-generated invoice numbers
  • Saved client profiles for fast invoicing
  • Mark invoices as paid — auto-updates revenue
  • Category-specific expense forms: Salaries, Rent, Supplies, Utilities, Transport, Marketing, Equipment
  • P&L tracker with gross profit, net profit, before and after tax
  • VAT and corporate tax reserve section
